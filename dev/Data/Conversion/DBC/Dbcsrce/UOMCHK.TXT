. UOMCHK/TEXT  ROUTINE FOR EXTENSIONS
...............................................................
. 
CHK100X  MATCH     "C " TO UOM
         GOTO      CHK1000 IF NOT EQUAL
         MOVE      PCSSOLD TO CALCFLD1
         DIVIDE    "100" INTO CALCFLD1
         MULT      LNDOLS BY CALCFLD1
         MOVE      CALCFLD1 TO LINECOST
         GOTO      DSPCOSTX
. 
CHK1000  MATCH     "M " TO UOM
         GOTO      EACHX   IF NOT EQUAL
         MOVE      PCSSOLD TO CALCFLD1
         DIVIDE    "1000"  INTO CALCFLD1
         MULT      LNDOLS  BY   CALCFLD1
         MOVE      CALCFLD1 TO   LINECOST
         GOTO      DSPCOSTX
. 
EACHX    MOVE      PCSSOLD TO LINECOST
         MULT      LNDOLS BY LINECOST
.
