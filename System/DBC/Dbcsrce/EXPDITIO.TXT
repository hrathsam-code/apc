.  EXPEDITIO/TEXT - I/O ROUTINES FOR EXPEDITE FUNCTIONS        .
................................................................
         
EXPOPEN  TRAPCLR   IO
         TRAP      NOEXP IF IO
.
         MOVE      "EXPEDITE"  TO  EXPNAME
         OPEN      EXPEDITE,EXPEDTNM
.
         MOVE      "EXACTINV"  TO  EXPNAME
         OPEN      EXACCTIN,EXACINNM
.
         MOVE      "EXACTPO"   TO  EXPNAME
         OPEN      EXACCTPO,EXACPONM
.
         MOVE      "H" TO IOSW
         RETURN
.
NOEXP    BEEP
         DISPLAY   *P1:23,*EL,"EXPEDITE FILE: ",EXPNAME," NOT ON LINE!":
                   *W2
         BEEP
         CHAIN     "ANSWER"
.
