. 
. THE MSPTXREF/ISI FILE	PROVIDES A CROSS-REFERENCE WHICH IS 
. USED TO INDICATE WHEN	A PART ENTERED ON THE MILSPEC PART FILE
. HAS AN EQUIVALENCY TO	THE PART NUMBER	OF ANOTHER SUPPLIER 
. 
. FOR ALL REPORTS AND INQUIRIES, THE MSXREFCD (CROSS REFERENCE CODE)
. LOCATED ON THE MSPARTFL/ISI MASTER RECORD, WILL BE INTERROGATED TO
. DETERMINE IF A CROSS-REFERENCE EXISTS....
. 
.      IF SO....THEN THE CROSS-REFERENCE PART NUMBER(S)	WILL BE	
.      RETRIEVED, AND DISPLAYED	AS INFORMATION RELATING	TO THE 
.      ORIGINALLY KEYED	MILSPEC	PART NUMBER
...........................................................................
.
